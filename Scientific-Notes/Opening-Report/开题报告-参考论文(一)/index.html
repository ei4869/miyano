    本报告为2025年2月27日更新的开题论文笔记，旨在为毕设研究提供参考。报告详细记录了关于相变存储器（PCRAM）和相变材料（PCM）的科研笔记，包括数学公式和理论模型。研究发现Cr2Ge2Te6材料因其极小的密度变化，适合高耐久性非易失性存储应用。实验验证了Cr2Ge2Te6薄膜在非晶态和晶态下的厚度变化极小，强调了在电存储器应用中，小的密度变化比大光学对比度更为重要。此外，报告还讨论了垂直-侧向H-TaS2/1T-TaS2异质结构，实现了室温附近的多态电阻转换和手性切换，为非易失性多级存储和手性调控提供新框架。综述了PCM在节能光子存储与计算中的应用，通过光学或电学脉冲加热PCM，触发其相变，改变光学和电学性质，实现光传输调制和编程方案。最后，介绍了基于PCM的光学-电混合模式存储单元，模拟生物突触工作机制，实现信号调制和记忆功能，具有高效能和低能耗的优势。

    # Systematic materials design for phase-change memory with small density changes for high-endurance non-volatile memory applications

    系统化材料设计用于相变存储器，以实现高耐久性非易失性存储应用中的小密度变化。

    1. 研究背景
      相变存储器（PCRAM）作为下一代非易失性存储技术的重要候选之一，面临耐久性（即存储单元的可擦写次数）的挑战。耐久性问题主要源于存储材料在晶态和非晶态之间转换时的密度变化，导致界面应力积累。
    2. 研究目的
      通过系统性的材料设计，寻找在相变过程中密度变化较小的材料，以提高 PCRAM 的耐久性。
    3. 研究方法
      利用密度泛函理论（DFT）模拟，结合经验定律（密度变化与光学对比度之间的关系），预测了多种过渡金属硫族化合物的密度变化。
    4. 主要发现
      Cr2Ge2Te6 被预测为一种具有极小密度变化的材料，适合用于高耐久性的非易失性存储应用。
    5. 实验验证
      通过实验测量了 Cr2Ge2Te6 薄膜在非晶态和晶态下的厚度变化，结果支持了模拟预测，显示出极小的密度变化。

    Some of present authors have found a clear correlation between the density change, Δρ/ρ, and optical reflectivity contrast, ΔR/R, in various PCMs.
    当前的一些作者已经发现，在各种相变材料（PCMs）中，密度变化（Δρ/ρ）与光学反射率对比度（ΔR/R）之间存在明显的相关性。

    It should be noted that many potential PCMs may have been overlooked because materials showing a smaller density change also exhibit a smaller optical contrast causing them to fall outside the scope of investigations for optical disc applications. For electrical memory applications,however, the criterion of large optical contrast between amorphous and crystalline phases is unnecessary, but rather instead, smaller density changes are much more important.
    需要注意的是，许多潜在的相变材料（PCMs）可能被忽视了，因为那些密度变化较小的材料通常也表现出较小的光学对比度，这使得它们超出了光盘应用研究范围。然而，对于电存储器应用而言，非晶态和晶态之间的大光学对比度标准并非必要，相反，较小的密度变化则显得更为重要。

    To apply theempiricalCMR, i.e. todetermine the relationshipbetweenΔρ/ρ andΔR/R, informationon the optical reflectivity of both the amorphous and crystalline phases is necessary to derive the corresponding density change Δρ/ρ.
    为了应用经验 CMR（即确定 Δρ/ρ 与 ΔR/R 之间的关系），需要了解非晶态和晶态的光学反射率信息，以便推导出相应的密度变化 Δρ/ρ。

    A series of PCM films were grown by sputtering onto Si single-crystal wafers with a thermally oxidized SiO2 toplayer.Deposition was carried out at RT resulting in the as-deposited films forming in the amorphous phase,with subsequent annealing transforming thefilms into the crystalline phase.
    采用热氧化 SiO2 表面层在单晶硅片上溅射法制备了一系列相变材料薄膜。在室温下沉积薄膜，使薄膜在非晶相中形成，随后退火使薄膜转变为晶相。

    Figure 1 shows the reflectivity spectra as a function of wavelength for the amorphous phase.
    图 1 显示了非晶相的反射率光谱作为波长的函数。

    Before doing so, it should be noted that DFT is a very powerful technique to predict the dielectric functions of materials, given the crystal structure.
    在此之前，需要指出的是，密度泛函理论（DFT）是一种非常强大的技术，能够在已知晶体结构的情况下预测材料的介电函数。

    we show how well the DFT simulations reproduce the experimental reflectivity of the crystalline phases for different PCMs.
    我们展示了 DFT 模拟如何很好地再现不同相变材料（PCMs）晶相的实验反射率。

    Figures 3(a) and 3(b) show the simulated reflectivity spectra for Ge-Te-X and Sb-Te-X,respectively. It was found that the spectra depend on the compounds and that the reflectivity values vary between 34%–68% at 630nm for Ge-Te-X and from 48%–73% for the Sb-Te-X system,respectively.
    图 3 (a) 和 3 (b) 分别展示了 Ge-Te-X 和 Sb-Te-X 的模拟反射光谱。研究发现，光谱特性取决于化合物成分，其中 Ge-Te-X 在 630nm 波长处的反射率在 34% 至 68% 之间变化，而 Sb-Te-X 体系的反射率则在 48% 至 73% 范围内。

    Figure4 indicates the correlation between density change and optical contrast for various PCMs.The predicted values of this work are plotted as red diamond symbols.The blue circles represent experimental data and are taken from the literature.The error bars along the x-axis are a consequence of the previously mentioned variation in the amorphous reflectivity, Ramo , 40.7% ± 2.8% at 630 nm. The error bars along the y-axis reflect the observed deviation from a linear fit curve obtained from experimental values (blue circles). The inset in Fig. 4 shows a magnified region in which a number of compounds have been plotted. This figure clearly shows that the estimated density change of various transition-metal-based tellurides varies from −2% to +6%.
    图 4 展示了不同相变材料（PCMs）的密度变化与光学对比度之间的相关性。本研究的预测值以红色菱形符号表示。蓝色圆圈代表实验数据，这些数据取自文献。
    x 轴上的误差条是前述非晶态反射率变化的结果，Ramo 在 630 nm 处为 40.7% ± 2.8%。y 轴上的误差条反映了实验值（蓝色圆圈）与线性拟合曲线的观测偏差。图 4 中的插图展示了一个放大的区域，其中绘制了多种化合物的数据。该图清晰地表明，各种基于过渡金属的碲化物的估计密度变化范围在−2% 到 + 6% 之间。

    Since a large optical contrast is required for optical disc applications, conventional PCMs such as GST and GeTe possess an optical contrast of more than 0.3, resulting in density changes of more than 6%. This is clear evidence as to why GST has been a good material for optical disc applications.
    由于光盘应用需要较大的光学对比度，传统的相变材料（PCMs）如 GST 和 GeTe 的光学对比度超过 0.3，导致密度变化超过 6%。这清楚地解释了为什么 GST 一直是光盘应用的理想材料。

    However, a smaller density change is more desirable for electrical memory applications. Assuming that a density change of less than ±2.5% is desired, we conclude that suitable materials for high-endurance PCM applications include Nb3GeTe6, ZrGeTe4, Fe2GeTe4, HfGeTe4, Mn2GeTe4, Cr2Ge2Te6 and Cu2GeTe3. In particular, Cr2Ge2Te6 is predicted to exhibit a very small density change, making this material promising for electrical memory applications.
    然而，对于电存储应用来说，更小的密度变化更为理想。假设希望密度变化小于 ±2.5%，我们得出结论，适合高耐久性 PCM 应用的材料包括 Nb3GeTe6、ZrGeTe4、Fe2GeTe4、HfGeTe4、Mn2GeTe4、Cr2Ge2Te6 和 Cu2GeTe3。特别是，Cr2Ge2Te6 预计会表现出非常小的密度变化，这使得这种材料在电存储应用中具有潜力.

    We chose Cr2Ge2Te6 as it is expected to show the smallest density change; Cu-doped GeTe (Cu5.1Ge46.2Te48.7) was used as a control sample.
    我们选择 Cr2Ge2Te6，因为它预计会显示出最小的密度变化；掺杂铜的 GeTe（Cu5.1Ge46.2Te48.7）被用作对照样品。

    The CuGeTe film shows a reduction in thickness after crystallization from 204.3 to 193.4 nm, which is equivalent to a density change of +5.3%. On the other hand, the measured thickness change is almost negligible for Cr2Ge2Te6 with a value of about −0.4%.
    CuGeTe 薄膜在结晶后厚度从 204.3 纳米减少到 193.4 纳米，相当于密度变化为 + 5.3%。另一方面，对于 Cr2Ge2Te6，测得的厚度变化几乎可以忽略不计，约为 - 0.4%。

    Based upon these observations, we would like to reiterate how this process (Fig. 4) is useful for finding novel materials for PCRAM applications with emphasis, in particular, on high endurance. Cr2Ge2Te6 memory devices as well as N-doped Cr2Ge2Te6 were shown to switch with much lower power consumption than GST. Therefore, a cycle test of a Cr2Ge2Te6 memory cell will be necessary in future for verification of high-endurance performance.
    基于这些观察，我们想重申这个过程（图 4）如何有助于发现用于 PCRAM 应用的新型材料，特别是强调高耐久性。Cr2Ge2Te6 存储器件以及掺杂氮的 Cr2Ge2Te6 显示出比 GST 更低的功耗。因此，未来需要进行 Cr2Ge2Te6 存储单元的循环测试，以验证其高耐久性能。

    Of course, our results do not exclude the possibility of binary transition-metal tellurides for non-volatile memory applications. MoTe2, a representative transition-metal dichalcogenide, has been predicted theoretically to show electrostatically-induced phase transitions with low energy consumption.
    当然，我们的结果并不排除二元过渡金属碲化物在非易失性存储应用中的可能性。MoTe2 作为一种代表性的过渡金属二硫化物，理论上预测会表现出低能耗的静电诱导相变。

    In conclusion, we have performed systematic materials design of novel transition-metal-based tellurides for PCRAM applications from the viewpoint of minimizing the density change upon a phase transition. The simulation results clearly demonstrate the variation of the density change depends on the compound, and Cr2Ge2Te6 was predicted to be the best PCM candidate owing to its small density change. This hypothesis was confirmed experimentally in that a Cr2Ge2Te6 film showed a negligible thickness change between the amorphous and crystalline phases.
    总之，我们从最小化相变时密度变化的角度，对新型过渡金属基碲化物进行了系统的材料设计，用于 PCRAM 应用。模拟结果清楚地表明，密度变化因化合物而异，Cr2Ge2Te6 由于其小的密度变化被预测为最佳的 PCM 候选材料。这一假设通过实验得到了证实，即 Cr2Ge2Te6 薄膜在非晶态和晶态之间的厚度变化可以忽略不计。

    # Encoding multistate charge order and chirality in endotaxial heterostructures

    在内延异质结构中编码多态电荷序与手性

    1. 研究背景
      高密度相变存储器（PCM）材料需要具有多个中间电阻态，这在 1T-TaS2 中由于电荷密度波（CDW）相变而被观察到。然而，这种亚稳态行为使得 TaS2 器件中的多态切换难以预测。
    2. 研究目的
      本文展示了通过制备纳米厚度的垂直 - 侧向 H-TaS2/1T-TaS2 异质结构，其中内含的金属 H-TaS2 单层数量决定了 1T-TaS2 层在室温附近的电阻转换次数。
    3. 研究方法
      通过热退火处理 1T-TaS2 晶体，引入单层 H-TaS2，形成垂直 - 侧向异质结构。利用选区电子衍射（SAED）、拉曼光谱、原子分辨率差分相位对比扫描透射电子显微镜（DPC-STEM）和四维扫描透射电子显微镜（4D-STEM）等技术对结构和性质进行表征。
    4. 主要发现
      观察到光学活性的异手性 CDW 超晶格结构，与电阻台阶同步调制，并展示了应变工程如何用于触发这些多型转换。这些异质结构在室温下表现出可预测的多态电阻和手性切换，为可靠的、非易失性的、多级存储和手性调控提供了新的框架。

    High-density phase change memory (PCM) storage is proposed for materials with multiple intermediate resistance states, which have been observed in 1T-TaS2 due to charge density wave (CDW) phase transitions. However, the metastability responsible for this behavior makes the presence of multistate switching unpredictable in TaS2 devices. Here, we demonstrate the fabrication of nanothick verti-lateral H-TaS2/1T-TaS2 heterostructures in which the number of endotaxial metallic H-TaS2 monolayers dictates the number of resistance transitions in 1T-TaS2 lamellae near room temperature. Further, we also observe optically active heterochirality in the CDW superlattice structure, which is modulated in concert with the resistivity steps, and we show how strain engineering can be used to nucleate these polytype conversions. This work positions the principle of endotaxial heterostructures as a promising conceptual framework for reliable, non-volatile, and multi-level switching of structure, chirality, and resistance.
    高密度相变存储器（PCM）存储被提议用于具有多种中间电阻态的材料，这些材料在 1T-TaS2 中由于电荷密度波（CDW）相变而被观察到。然而，导致这种行为的亚稳性使得 TaS2 器件中的多态切换变得不可预测。在这里，我们展示了纳米厚度的垂直横向 H-TaS2/1T-TaS2 异质结构的制造，其中内含的金属 H-TaS2 单层的数量决定了 1T-TaS2 层状结构在室温附近的电阻转变次数。此外，我们还在 CDW 超晶格结构中观察到了光学活性的异手性，这种异手性与电阻率台阶同步变化，我们展示了如何利用应变工程来引发这些多型转变。这项工作将内含异质结构的原理定位为一个有前景的概念框架，用于实现可靠、非易失性和多级的结构、手性和电阻切换。

    The CDW materials host correlated electronic states typified by periodic lattice distortions and static modulations of conduction electrons. Non-volatile memory and computing devices based on the principle of phase change memory (PCM) may leverage the intrinsic resistivity changes associated with CDW phase transitions. 1T-TaS2, a van der Waals (vdW) layered solid, is a prototypical CDW material in which the atomic lattice distorts in-plane to form 13-atom star-shaped clusters.
    CDW 材料具有周期性晶格畸变和传导电子的静态调制所特有的相关电子态。基于相变存储器（PCM）原理的非易失性存储和计算设备可能会利用与 CDW 相变相关的固有电阻变化。1T-TaS2 是一种范德华（vdW）层状固体，是典型的 CDW 材料，其原子晶格在平面内畸变形成 13 原子星形簇。

    In this work, we demonstrate an approach converse to preceding literature—employing moderate thermal annealing to interdisperse monolayer H-TaS2 between few-layer 1T-TaS2 lamellae. In the resulting verti-lateral 1T-TaS2/H-TaS2 heterostructures, decoupled 1T-TaS2 fragments undergo independent transitions from the disordered incommensurate (IC) to ordered commensurate CDW phase above room temperature. These transitions are hallmarked by synchronous stepwise switching of chirality and resistance with high predictability; the number of steps is encoded by the quantity and arrangement of H-TaS2 layers.
    在这项工作中，我们展示了一种与以往文献相反的方法 —— 利用适度的热退火将单层 H-TaS2 分散在几层 1T-TaS2 层状结构之间。在所得到的垂直横向 1T-TaS2/H-TaS2 异质结构中，解耦的 1T-TaS2 片段在室温以上独立地从无序的非公度（IC）相变到有序的公度 CDW 相。这些转变以手性和电阻的同步阶梯式切换为特征，具有高度的可预测性；步骤的数量由 H-TaS2 层的数量和排列所编码。

    To unveil the relationship between ∆OCR, CDW commensuration and atomic lattice structure of these annealed 1T-TaS2 crystals, atomic resolution differential-phase-contrast scanning transmission electron microscopy (DPC-STEM) imaging and analysis was performed on cross-sectional samples made from optically distinct regions of flakes S1 and S2. Representative DPC-STEM micrographs reveal that thermal annealing induces a partial transition from the 1T (octahedrally coordinated Ta) to the H (trigonal prismatic Ta) structure. The H polytype forms within the 1T-TaS2 matrix (i.e., endotaxially), overwhelmingly as monolayers, separating generally thicker fragments of 1T-TaS2.
    为了揭示这些退火后的 1T-TaS2 晶体的∆OCR、CDW 公度性和原子晶格结构之间的关系，对从光学上不同的区域制备的 S1 和 S2 薄片的横截面样品进行了原子分辨率的差分相位对比扫描透射电子显微镜（DPC-STEM）成像和分析。典型的 DPC-STEM 显微图像显示，热退火诱导了从 1T（八面体配位的 Ta）到 H（三方柱状 Ta）结构的部分转变。H 多型在 1T-TaS2 基体中形成（即内含地），主要是单层，分隔出通常更厚的 1T-TaS2 片段。

    The ensemble room-temperature ordering of CDW domains in TaS2 heterostructures with different polytype compositions was further probed using SAED. Our findings support that samples comprising ≥ 20% of H-TaS2 manifest an ordered C-CDW phase, characterized by sharp CDW reflections.
    进一步使用 SAED 探测了具有不同多型组成的 TaS2 异质结构中 CDW 畴在室温下的整体有序性。我们的发现支持这样的观点，即包含≥20% H-TaS2 的样品表现出有序的 C-CDW 相，其特征是尖锐的 CDW 反射。

    The heterochirality of CDW superlattices in H-TaS2/1T-TaS2 at room temperature was mapped with nano-scale resolution using four-dimensional scanning transmission electron microscopy (4D-STEM). In 4D-STEM, a converged electron probe is scanned across a sample in a 2D array, while recording 2D diffraction data at each probe position.
    利用四维扫描透射电子显微镜（4D-STEM）以纳米级分辨率绘制了 H-TaS2/1T-TaS2 中 CDW 超晶格在室温下的异手性。在 4D-STEM 中，一个汇聚的电子探针在二维阵列中扫描样品，同时在每个探针位置记录二维衍射数据。

    The chirality of TaS2 heterostructures can also be assessed optically, as enantiomorphic disproportion engenders Raman optical activity (ROA): a distinct Raman response to right and left-circularly polarized light. For 1T-TaS2 displaying ROA, the integrated area ratio of Eg(I) and Eg(II) modes differs in the σ+σ− and σ−σ+ Raman polarization configurations.
    TaS2 异质结构的手性也可以通过光学方法评估，因为对映体比例失衡会产生拉曼光学活性（ROA）：对右旋和左旋圆偏振光有明显的拉曼响应。对于表现出 ROA 的 1T-TaS2，Eg (I) 和 Eg (II) 模式的积分面积比在 σ+σ−和 σ−σ+ 拉曼偏振配置中不同。

    Electronic properties of these heterochiral endotaxial polytype heterostructures were probed using variable-temperature transport measurements. In these studies, we monitored the temperature-dependent longitudinal resistance (Rxx) of mesoscopic devices fabricated from S1 and S4.
    这些异手性内含多型异质结构的电子性质通过变温输运测量来探测。在这些研究中，我们监测了从 S1 和 S4 制备的介观器件的温度依赖纵向电阻（Rxx）。

    The formation of H-TaS2 layers dictates the stepwise evolution of resistance and chirality with temperature in these endotaxial polytype heterostructures. Upon cooling, we observe a series of stepped resistance increases, with the step count matching the number of 1T-TaS2 slabs determined from DPC-STEM images of device cross-sections.
    H-TaS2 层的形成决定了这些内含多型异质结构中电阻和手性随温度的逐步演变。在冷却过程中，我们观察到一系列阶梯式的电阻增加，其步数与从器件横截面的 DPC-STEM 图像确定的 1T-TaS2 层板数量相匹配。

    Lastly, having established the structure and multistate electronic/chiral switching in TaS2 endotaxial heterostructures, we turn to considering the mechanism of nucleation of these polytype transformations, finding them to be facilitated by wrinkles and folds.
    最后，在确定了 TaS2 内含异质结构的结构和多态电子 / 手性切换之后，我们转向考虑这些多型转变的成核机制，发现这些转变是由皱纹和褶皱促进的。

    We build upon these nanoscale insights to demonstrate that mechanical/strain engineering of 1T-TaS2 may be used for rational design of vertical/lateral TaS2 heterostructures. To this end, we stacked a 14-layer 1T-TaS2 crystal onto a ∼ 112 nm-thick hexagonal boron nitride (hBN) flake to deliberately impart local stress onto the region of the 1T-TaS2 flake in the vicinity of hBN.
    我们基于这些纳米级的见解，证明了 1T-TaS2 的机械 / 应变工程可用于垂直 / 横向 TaS2 异质结构的合理设计。为此，我们将一个 14 层的 1T-TaS2 晶体堆叠到一个约 112 纳米厚的六方氮化硼（hBN）薄片上，以故意在 hBN 附近的 1T-TaS2 薄片区域施加局部应力。

    # Phase‑change materials for energy‑efficient photonic memory and computing

    相变材料在节能光子存储与计算中的应用

    1. 研究目的
      本文综述了相变材料（PCM）在能量高效的光子存储和计算中的应用，特别是将 PCM 存储单元与集成光电子学相结合的进展。
    2. 相变机制
      通过光学或电学脉冲加热 PCM，触发其在无定形（amorphous）和晶态（crystalline）之间的相变，从而改变其光学和电学性质。
    3. 光电子波导存储器单元的工作原理
    • 光传输调制：在光电子波导设备上沉积 PCM 薄膜，通过相变调制光的传输。例如，4um 长、10nm 厚的非晶态 Ge2Sb2Te5（aGST）和晶态 Ge2Sb2Te5（cGST）薄膜的光传输从 95% 降低到 26%。
    • 编程方案：使用光学脉冲和电学脉冲来切换 PCM 的相态。短而高幅度的脉冲用于熔融淬火（amorphization），长而低幅度的脉冲用于退火（crystallization）。
    1. 光学编程的 PCM 波导设备
    • 自由空间光学编程：通过远场自由空间光学脉冲切换 PCM，能量消耗较大，例如至少 900 pJ。
    • 波导光学编程：通过波导传输泵浦脉冲，实现平面内光与 PCM 的相互作用，吸收完全，编程能量降低。例如，使用皮秒（ps）光学脉冲编程，能量消耗低至 13.4 pJ，速度接近 1 GHz。
    1. 电学编程的 PCM 波导设备
    • 电学脉冲：通过焦耳加热（Joule heating）诱导 PCM 的相变。例如，使用离子注入硅波导微加热器，能量消耗为 10 nJ 和 9 nJ（amorphization 和 crystallization）。
    • 微加热器：通过在波导上覆盖电阻性 ITO 和石墨烯条带，实现对 PCM 的加热。石墨烯微加热器显示出比 ITO 和硅 PIN 微加热器更高的性能。
    1. 混合模式波导存储器单元
    • 混合模式操作：支持光学和电学编程及探测的非易失性光电子接口。例如，使用等离子体纳米间隙设备，通过表面等离子体极化激元（SPPs）增强光与物质的相互作用，降低编程能量。
    • 编程能量：等离子体纳米间隙设备的光学切换阈值仅为 (16 ± 2) pJ。
    1. 性能比较
    • 能量消耗：使用近场波导光学编程，能量消耗从 nJ 级降低到 pJ 级。例如，使用 ps 泵浦脉冲，能量消耗为 60 pJ/190 pJ，切换对比度为 20%，编程速度接近 GHz。
    • 电学编程：实现 300% 的对比度，能量消耗为几 nJ。例如，使用微加热器，可以对大面积 PCM 进行切换。
    1. 内存中光子张量核心
    • 光子张量核心：基于能量高效的光电子波导存储器单元，开发大规模存储芯片，实现内存中计算，如光子张量核心，用于执行乘积累加（MAC）操作和矩阵 - 向量乘法（MVM）。
    • 并行处理：通过波长分复用（WDM），实现多个输入的同时处理，提高计算密度和效率。

    In this article, we overview recent progress in this direction that integrates phase-change material (PCM) memory elements with integrated optoelectronics. We compare performances of PCM devices using optoelectronic programming schemes and show that energy consumption can be significantly reduced to 60 pJ using picosecond (ps) optical pulse programming and plasmonic nanogap devices with a programming speed approaching 1 GHz. With these energy-efficient waveguide memories, concepts of in-memory photonic computing are implemented based on crossbar arrays. Compared with digital electronic accelerators: application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs) and graphics processing units (GPUs), photonic cores promise 1−3 orders higher compute density and energy efficiency, although much more work toward commercialization is still required.
    在本文中，我们综述了将相变材料（PCM）存储元件与集成光电子器件相结合的最新进展。我们比较了采用光电编程方案的 PCM 器件性能，并展示了通过皮秒（ps）光脉冲编程和等离子体纳米间隙器件，能耗可显著降低至 60 pJ，编程速度接近 1 GHz。借助这些高能效波导存储器，基于交叉阵列的内存光子计算概念得以实现。与数字电子加速器（如专用集成电路 ASIC 和图形处理单元 GPU）相比，光子核心在计算密度和能效上提升了 1−3 个数量级，尽管实现商业化仍需更多努力。

    Phase transitions can be triggered by heating up the PCMs with optical or electrical pulse stimuli. It allows precise tailoring of the optical and electrical properties of the materials. In the electronic domain, in-memory computing is achieved by storing analogue conductance values in the PCM cells, which are commonly arranged in a crossbar array. Implementations in the electronic domain, however, suffer from limitations in bandwidth and data throughput.
    相变可以通过光或电脉冲刺激加热 PCM 来触发，从而精确调控材料的光学和电学特性。在电子领域，内存计算通过在 PCM 单元中存储模拟电导值实现，这些单元通常排列成交叉阵列。然而，电子领域的实现受限于带宽和数据吞吐量。

    Importantly, performing linear operations such as multiply accumulate (MAC) and matrix–vector multiplication (MVM) in the optical domain not only improves the speed, but also consumes considerably less energy.
    重要的是，在光域执行线性操作，如乘积累加（MAC）和矩阵向量乘法（MVM），不仅提高了速度，还大幅降低了能耗。

    In the optical domain, transmission of light can be modulated by the structural phase transition of the PCM thin film deposited on top of an optoelectronic waveguide device (as shown in the schematic in Figure 1b).
    在光域中，光的传输可以通过沉积在光电波导器件顶部的 PCM 薄膜的结构相变来调制（如图 1b 示意图所示）。

    The PCM, while nearly lossless in its amorphous state, becomes absorptive upon phase transition to the crystalline state.
    在无定形态下几乎无损的相变材料（PCM），在转变为晶态时会变得具有吸收性。

    In this optical memory cell, the signal information is stored as the PCM structural phase.
    在这个光学存储单元中，信号信息以 PCM 的结构相态存储。

    To switch the structural state of the PCM thin film, optical pulses (red) in the waveguide/free-space and electrical pulses (yellow) are sent to the photonic device.
    为了切换 PCM 薄膜的结构状态，波导 / 自由空间中的光脉冲（红色）和电脉冲（黄色）被发送到光子器件中。

    When an optical pulse is sent, the PCM thin film absorbs the optical energy and generates heat. When an electrical pulse is sent instead, the net current from the electrical pulse passes through an ion-implanted resistive region in a silicon waveguide, and thus generates heat. Upon phase transition by pulse heating, the signal modulation (attenuation) induced by the different PCM states can be applied to mimic the workings of a biological synapse, which likewise, contain both synaptic plasticity modulation and memory functions. The PCM states are highly stable for years at room temperature.
    当发送光脉冲时，PCM 薄膜吸收光能并产生热量。当发送电脉冲时，电脉冲的净电流通过硅波导中的离子注入电阻区域，从而产生热量。通过脉冲加热实现相变后，不同 PCM 状态引起的信号调制（衰减）可用于模拟生物突触的工作机制，同样包含突触可塑性调制和记忆功能。PCM 状态在室温下可保持多年高度稳定。

    Generally, a short and high-amplitude optical/electrical pulse is used to melt-quench (i.e., temperature sharply rising above the melting temperature (Tm) and quickly dissipates to room temperature) the PCM to an amorphous state as shown in Figure 1d. In contrast, a longer and lower amplitude optical/electrical pulse is used to anneal the PCM with temperature ramping up between Tm and transition temperature (Tx) for a certain period (to recrystallize the atomic lattice), before finally ramping the temperature down to room temperature.
    通常，使用短而高幅度的光 / 电脉冲将 PCM 熔融淬火（即温度迅速升至熔化温度（Tm）以上并迅速降至室温）至无定形态，如图 1d 所示。相比之下，使用较长且较低幅度的光 / 电脉冲对 PCM 进行退火处理，温度在 Tm 和转变温度（Tx）之间逐渐上升一段时间（以重新结晶原子晶格），最后将温度降至室温。

    In contrast to free-space optical switching, sending pump pulses along the waveguide leads to in-plane light–PCM interaction with perfect absorption and reduced programming energies.
    与自由空间光学切换相比，沿波导发送泵浦脉冲可实现平面内光 - PCM 相互作用，具有完美吸收和降低的编程能量。

    In addition to optical programming, electrical stimulus can also induce phase transition of PCMs based on Joule heating.
    除了光学编程外，电刺激也可以通过焦耳加热诱导 PCM 的相变。

    As for other waveguide platforms (e.g., SiC, Si3N4, LiNbO3) with large bandgaps, microheaters can be achieved by capping resistive ITO29 and graphene strips30 on top of waveguides to heat up PCMs as shown in Figure 3d–e, respectively. A recent study predicts that graphene microheaters display two orders of magnitude higher figure of merits (FOMs) for heating and overall performance compared with those of ITO and silicon PIN microheaters.
    对于其他具有大带隙的波导平台（如 SiC、Si3N4、LiNbO3），可以通过在波导顶部覆盖电阻性 ITO29 和石墨烯条 30 来实现微加热器，以加热 PCM，如图 3d-e 所示。最近的一项研究预测，石墨烯微加热器的加热和整体性能的优值（FOMs）比 ITO 和硅 PIN 微加热器高出两个数量级。

    Here, the state of the PCM can be programmed using optical or electrical pulses and can be further read simultaneously in the optical and electrical domains. Its working principle is detailed in Figure 4a. Optical programming of the mixed-mode memory cell is based on the absorption of optical pulses by the PCM. Tailoring the fraction of amorphous-to-crystalline volume of the PCM results in a nonvolatile change in electrical resistance and optical transmission. In electrical programming, a conductive filament (cPCM) in a PCM element may be either formed or broken between the two gold pads based on either the threshold switching or Joule heating and subsequent melt-quenching of the cGST filament.
    在这里，PCM 的状态可以通过光脉冲或电脉冲进行编程，并且可以在光学和电学域中同时读取。其工作原理如图 4a 所示。混合模式存储单元的光学编程基于 PCM 对光脉冲的吸收。调整 PCM 中无定形与晶态体积的比例会导致电阻和光传输的非易失性变化。在电编程中，PCM 元件中的导电细丝（cPCM）可以在两个金垫之间形成或断裂，基于阈值切换或焦耳加热以及随后的 cGST 细丝熔融淬火。

    Thus, performances of photonic memory cells may be further improved by exploiting both plasmonic enhancement and ultrashort optical pulse programming. Electrical programming is also promising for achieving large contrasts (300%) with energy consumption of a few nJ.11 In this scenario, a large area of PCM can be switched by a single electrical pulse.
    因此，通过利用等离子体增强和超短光脉冲编程，光子存储单元的性能可以进一步提高。电编程在实现大对比度（300%）和低能耗（几纳焦耳）方面也很有前景。在这种情况下，单个电脉冲可以切换大面积 PCM。

    Based on the previously discussed energy-efficient optoelectronic waveguide memory cells, the development of large-scale memory chips is more promising as these photonic memory cells can be used to implement in-memory computing, such as a photonic tensor core for performing multiply accumulate (MAC) operations and matrix–vector multiplications (MVMs) using photonic integrated circuits.
    基于之前讨论的节能光电波导存储单元，大规模存储芯片的发展更具前景，因为这些光子存储单元可用于实现内存计算，例如使用光子集成电路执行乘加（MAC）操作和矩阵 - 向量乘法（MVMs）的光子张量核心。

    Compared with digital electronic accelerator (ASIC and GPU), the photonic core has 1−3 orders of magnitude improvement in both compute density and efficiency.
    与数字电子加速器（ASIC 和 GPU）相比，光子核心在计算密度和效率上提高了 1-3 个数量级

    Waveguide memory cells leverage the near-field interaction to reduce energy consumption. Crucially, energy consumption is only 60 pJ in an all-optical abacus with operating speed approaching 1 GHz.24 And optical switching threshold is drastically reduced to (16 ± 2) pJ in a plasmonic nanogap device.
    波导存储单元利用近场相互作用来降低能耗。关键的是，在全光学算盘中，能耗仅为 60 pJ，操作速度接近 1 GHz。在等离子体纳米间隙器件中，光学切换阈值显著降低至（16 ± 2）pJ。
