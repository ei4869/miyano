    本文探讨了钙钛矿结构锶钴氧化物SrCoO3-δ（P-SCO）的制备和相变过程，并提出了两种新方法：碱（AAG）和酸溶液（ASR）触发的拓扑相变，将布朗米勒结构SrCoO2.5（BM-SCO）转化为P-SCO。通过脉冲激光沉积技术制备BM-SCO薄膜，并利用原位XRD研究了AAG方法下的相变，发现AAG在-0.6V电压下约2分钟内触发相变，而ASR方法在室温下仅需2秒完成。实验和DFT计算证实了两种方法的有效性，并揭示了ASR中的质子辅助离子歧化反应机制。为了验证这一机制，研究者使用ICP-MS测量了硫酸溶液中的元素浓度，将BM-SCO薄膜浸泡在硫酸中15秒后，发现溶液中的Co离子含量与基于质子辅助离子歧化模型的理论值相吻合。P-SCO薄膜的磁性和电学特性表明成功从绝缘BM-SCO转变为导电P-SCO，其中AAG样品接近金属特性，ASR样品表现为半导体行为。通过异位臭氧退火（EOA）方法获得的P-SCO样品表现出不同的c晶格常数和氧含量变化，以及不同的磁性和电学特性。AAG和ASR方法都能有效将BM-SCO转化为导电P-SCO，且与Co离子价态和氧空位浓度密切相关，为理解P-SCO的磁性和电学特性提供了新见解，并具有科研参考价值。

    # Facile Pathways to Synthesize Perovskite Strontium Cobalt Oxides

    合成钙钛矿型锶钴氧化物的简易途径

    1. 研究背景
      钙钛矿结构的锶钴氧化物（P-SCO）因其独特的磁性和电子性质而受到广泛关注。然而，合成高价位的 P-SCO 具有挑战性，因为其更高的吉布斯自由能和氧空位形成能。
    2. 研究目的
      本文揭示了两种新的方法，通过水溶液中的碱（AAG）和酸溶液（ASR）触发从布朗米勒结构的 SrCoO2.5（BM-SCO）到钙钛矿结构的 SrCoO3（P-SCO）的拓扑相变。
    3. 研究方法
      使用脉冲激光沉积技术制备了高质量的 BM-SCO 薄膜，并通过原位电压依赖的 X 射线衍射（XRD）研究了 AAG 方法下的相变。同时，通过酸溶液反应（ASR）方法实现了快速相变。
    4. 主要发现
      AAG 方法在 - 0.6 V 的临界电压下触发相变，在 - 1 V 的栅极电压下，相变时间约为 2 分钟。ASR 方法在室温下仅需 2 秒即可触发相变。通过 XRD、磁性和电学特性测量，证实了这两种方法都能将 BM-SCO 成功转化为 P-SCO。密度泛函理论（DFT）计算揭示了 ASR 方法中的质子辅助离子歧化反应机制。

    Using brownmillerite SrCoO2.5 (BM-SCO), two novel pathways are revealed to achieve the topotactic phase transition into its corresponding perovskite SrCoO3 (P-SCO). It is demonstrated that by using aqueous alkali as the electrolyte during gating, the negative biased voltage triggers a rapid transition into P-SCO, which is attributed to the presence of strong oxidizing hydroxyl radicals. While surprisingly, it is observed that the acid solution with rich protons can also trigger an unexpected phase transition from BM-SCO into P-SCO in a much faster manner. With density functional theory calculations, this transition is elucidated as a proton-assist ionic disproportionation, in which the Co ions are simultaneously oxidized and reduced, while the latter one is dissolved within the solution.
    利用布朗米勒石结构的 SrCoO2.5（BM-SCO），揭示了两种新颖的途径来实现其向钙钛矿结构 SrCoO3（P-SCO）的拓扑相变。研究表明，在栅极控制过程中使用碱性水溶液作为电解质时，负偏压会触发快速向 P-SCO 的转变，这归因于强氧化性羟基自由基的存在。然而，令人惊讶的是，富含质子的酸性溶液也能以更快的速度引发从 BM-SCO 到 P-SCO 的意外相变。通过密度泛函理论计算，这一相变被解释为质子辅助的离子歧化反应，其中钴离子同时被氧化和还原，而后者则溶解在溶液中。

    Strontium cobalt oxides are a model system of multivalence TMOs, in which a pronounced topotactic phase transition between brownmillerite SrCoO2.5 (BM-SCO)[10] and perovskite SrCoO3-δ (P-SCO),[11] drives a phase switching between insulating antiferromagnetic state and metallic ferromagnetic state, demonstrating an exotic magnetoelectric coupling,[12,13] and resistive switch.[14,15] During the studies, it was found that the synthesis of high-valence P-SCO is of fundamental challenge, because of the higher Gibbs free energy[16] and reduced oxygen vacancy formation energy of high-valence Co4+, especially for epitaxially strained thin films.[17,18] Many efforts have been devoted to synthesizing P-SCO from BM-SCO,[19–30] which could be classified into two categories, high-temperature annealing under an oxidative atmosphere and electrochemical redox reaction within an electrolyte.
    锶钴氧化物是多价过渡金属氧化物（TMOs）的模型体系，其中布朗米勒石结构的 SrCoO2.5（BM-SCO）与钙钛矿结构的 SrCoO3-δ（P-SCO）之间的显著拓扑相变，驱动了绝缘反铁磁态与金属铁磁态之间的相切换，展示了奇异的磁电耦合和电阻开关效应。在研究过程中，发现高价态 P-SCO 的合成具有根本性挑战，这是由于高价态 Co4 + 的高吉布斯自由能和较低的氧空位形成能，特别是对于外延应变的薄膜。许多努力致力于从 BM-SCO 合成 P-SCO，这些方法可分为两类：氧化气氛下的高温退火和电解质中的电化学氧化还原反应。

    In this work, we demonstrated two novel pathways to trigger the oxygen ionic evolution within BM-SCO, leading to the phase transition into P-SCO (Figure 1a). We revealed that the aqueous alkali gating (AAG) could trigger a rapid phase transition due to the presence of strong oxidizing hydroxyl radicals (Figure 1b). While surprisingly, the acid solution can also lead to a distinct structural transition into P-SCO phase, which was termed as acid solution reaction (ASR) method in this study (Figure 1c).
    在这项工作中，我们展示了两种新颖的途径来触发 BM-SCO 中的氧离子演化，从而引发向 P-SCO 的相变（图 1a）。我们揭示了碱性水溶液栅极控制（AAG）可以由于强氧化性羟基自由基的存在而触发快速相变（图 1b）。然而，令人惊讶的是，酸性溶液也能导致向 P-SCO 相的不同结构转变，在本研究中被称为酸溶液反应（ASR）方法（图 1c）。

    In this study, we first prepared high-quality BM-SCO thin films with a thickness of ≈30 nm through pulsed laser deposition on (001)-oriented (LaAlO3)0.3-(SrAl0.5Ta0.5O3)0.7 (LSAT, a = 3.868 Å) substrates (see Experimental Section). The as-prepared samples show an excellent crystalline quality with the atomic stacking of oxygen tetrahedra and octahedra along the film normal, as evidenced by the superlattice peaks of the X-ray diffraction (XRD) results.
    在本研究中，我们首先通过脉冲激光沉积在（001）取向的 (LaAlO3) 0.3-(SrAl0.5Ta0.5O3) 0.7（LSAT，a = 3.868 Å）衬底上制备了厚度约为 30 nm 的高质量 BM-SCO 薄膜（见实验部分）。所制备的样品显示出优异的晶体质量，氧四面体和八面体沿薄膜法线方向堆叠，X 射线衍射（XRD）结果中的超晶格峰证明了这一点。

    First, to verify the influence of the hydroxyl radical during the phase transition, we carried out similar gating measurements as the previous ILG study[12] while using the 0.1 m KOH solution as the liquid electrolyte. The in situ voltage dependence XRD studies reveal that the topotactic phase transition occurs at a critical voltage of −0.6 V, which is clearly lower than the value (i.e., −1.0 V) required for the ILG method[12] (Figure 2a). Furthermore, the phase transition time is ≈ 2 min with a gating voltage of −1 V, which is much faster than the previously employed ILG method even with a larger gating voltage.
    首先，为了验证羟基自由基在相变过程中的影响，我们进行了与之前离子液体栅极控制（ILG）研究类似的栅极测量，但使用 0.1 M KOH 溶液作为液体电解质。原位电压依赖性 XRD 研究表明，拓扑相变在临界电压 - 0.6 V 时发生，这明显低于 ILG 方法所需的电压（即 - 1.0 V）（图 2a）。此外，在 - 1 V 的栅极电压下，相变时间约为 2 分钟，这比之前使用的 ILG 方法快得多，即使使用了更大的栅极电压。

    We found that by simply soaking the BM-SCO thin film into an acid solution (36% acetic acid (HAc)) at room temperature for an extremely short time of 2 s, a distinct structural transition can be triggered, as shown in Figure 2c. This XRD result is very close to that obtained with the ILG and AAG methods.
    我们发现，通过将 BM-SCO 薄膜在室温下简单地浸泡在酸性溶液（36% 乙酸（HAc））中极短的时间（2 秒），可以触发明显的结构转变，如图 2c 所示。这一 XRD 结果与通过 ILG 和 AAG 方法获得的结果非常接近。

    We also measured the P-SCO sample obtained through the ex-situ ozone annealing (EOA) method, in which the BM-SCO was annealed in an ozone atmosphere at 300 °C for 40 min.[31] In the XRD results shown in Figure 2d, the as-grown BM-SCO thin film shows a series of characteristic superlattice peaks, which are attributed to the alternating stacking of the oxygen octahedra and tetrahedra.
    我们还测量了通过异位臭氧退火（EOA）方法获得的 P-SCO 样品，其中 BM-SCO 在 300°C 的臭氧气氛中退火 40 分钟（图 2d）。在 XRD 结果中，生长的 BM-SCO 薄膜显示出一系列特征超晶格峰，这些峰归因于氧八面体和四面体的交替堆叠。

    A detailed analysis of the XRD data (Figure 2d) reveals that these P-SCO samples show slightly different diffraction angles, indicating distinct c-lattice constant due to their varied oxygen contents, which is widely observed for P-SCO with an oxygen vacancy-induced lattice expansion.
    对 XRD 数据的详细分析（图 2d）显示，这些 P-SCO 样品显示出略微不同的衍射角，表明由于氧含量的变化，它们具有不同的 c 晶格常数，这在 P-SCO 中广泛观察到，由于氧空位引起的晶格膨胀。

    Distinct from the antiferromagnetic BM-SCO with zero magnetization, both P-SCO thin films show well-defined hysteresis loops, suggesting the development of a ferromagnetic state through the phase transition (Figure 3a). Notably, the sample obtained from the ASR method shows a slightly smaller saturated magnetization as compared with the one obtained from AAG method.
    与零磁化的反铁磁性 BM-SCO 不同，两种 P-SCO 薄膜都显示出明确的磁滞回线，表明通过相变形成了铁磁态（图 3a）。值得注意的是，通过 ASR 方法获得的样品显示出比通过 AAG 方法获得的样品略小的饱和磁化强度。

    To investigate the electrical properties of both P-SCO thin films, we carried out the temperature-dependent resistivity measurements as shown in Figure 3c. These two samples show a significantly reduced resistivity compared with the BM-SCO film, which is more than three orders of magnitude at room temperature. These results again indicate that both methods transform the insulating BM-SCO into conducting P-SCO. Specifically, the metallic property of the AAG sample is very close to that of the EOA sample, with a negligible temperature dependence, while the ASR sample shows a semiconducting behavior in which the resistivity increases with the decrease of the temperature.
    为了研究两种 P-SCO 薄膜的电学特性，我们进行了温度依赖的电阻率测量，如图 3c 所示。与 BM-SCO 薄膜相比，这两种样品的电阻率显著降低，室温下降低幅度超过三个数量级。这些结果再次表明，两种方法都将绝缘的 BM-SCO 转变为导电的 P-SCO。具体而言，AAG 样品的金属特性与 EOA 样品非常接近，温度依赖性可忽略不计，而 ASR 样品表现出半导体行为，电阻率随温度降低而增加。

    While for the sample obtained from AAG method, some small particles can be clearly visualized from the sample surface (Figure S3c, Supporting Information), which might be related to the electrochemical corrosion. Finally, for the sample obtained from ASR method, the sample shows a ravine-like feature (Figure S3d, Supporting Information), which could be attributed to the strong chemical reactions.
    对于通过 AAG 方法获得的样品，样品表面可以清晰地观察到一些小颗粒（图 S3c，支持信息），这可能与电化学腐蚀有关。最后，对于通过 ASR 方法获得的样品，样品显示出类似沟壑的特征（图 S3d，支持信息），这可以归因于强烈的化学反应。

    Therefore, the critical gating voltage (≈1.5 V)[12] is determined by the water electrolysis process. While, within the aqueous alkali of 0.1 m KOH solution, the presence of hydroxyl ions can turn into the hydroxyl radical and then molecular oxygen, the most reactive species of oxygen, through the electrochemical reactions. As shown in Figure S4a (Supporting Information), with a gating voltage of −1 V and deionized water as an electrolyte, no phase transition can be triggered with a gating duration of 10 min. However, by slightly increasing the concentration of KOH to 0.001 m, the structural transition can be readily triggered. Notably, the transition process becomes robust for even stronger alkali solutions (e.g., 0.1 m KOH).
    因此，临界栅极电压（≈1.5 V）[12] 由水电解过程决定。而在 0.1 m KOH 水溶液中，羟基离子的存在可以通过电化学反应转化为羟基自由基，然后转化为分子氧，即氧的最活泼形态。如图 S4a（支持信息）所示，在栅极电压为−1 V 且使用去离子水作为电解质时，栅极持续时间为 10 分钟时无法触发相变。然而，将 KOH 浓度略微增加至 0.001 m 时，结构转变即可轻松触发。值得注意的是，对于更强的碱溶液（如 0.1 m KOH），转变过程变得更加稳定。

    During this reaction, the abundant hydroxyl ions (OH−) within the KOH solution, as compared with that in the ionic liquid, promote the reaction rate, leading to reduced critical voltage and enhanced oxidation speed.
    在此反应过程中，KOH 溶液中丰富的羟基离子（OH−）与离子液体中的相比，促进了反应速率，导致临界电压降低和氧化速度加快。

    We found that for pH values >5, the phase transition is hardly observed, while for the solutions with lower (≤4) pH values, the phase transition occurs within 15 s (Figure S4b, Supporting Information). Interestingly, the reaction can occur in all tested acidic solutions (HAc, HCl, H2SO4, and even carbonated drinks) with enough proton concentration (pH value), regardless of the anions (Figure S4c, Supporting Information). Since the protons are very reductive, they would not trigger the oxidizing reaction alone.
    我们发现，当 pH 值 > 5 时，几乎观察不到相变，而对于 pH 值较低（≤4）的溶液，相变在 15 秒内发生（图 S4b，支持信息）。有趣的是，在所有测试的酸性溶液（HAc、HCl、H2SO4，甚至碳酸饮料）中，只要质子浓度（pH 值）足够，反应即可发生，而与阴离子无关（图 S4c，支持信息）。由于质子具有很强的还原性，它们单独不会触发氧化反应。

    In this process, the proton acts as the catalyst, which may reduce the oxygen adsorption and activation energies during the oxygen reduction reaction (ORR).[46] In this pathway, the source of oxygen should be attributed to the dissolved oxygen molecules within the solution.
    在此过程中，质子作为催化剂，可能降低了氧还原反应（ORR）过程中氧吸附和活化能。[46] 在此途径中，氧的来源应归因于溶液中溶解的氧分子。

    However, a negligible amount of 18O was determined (Figure 4a; Figure S5, Supporting Information), which can then safely exclude the participation of O2 molecules during this phase transition.
    然而，检测到的 18O 量可以忽略不计（图 4a；图 S5，支持信息），因此可以安全地排除 O2 分子在此相变过程中的参与。

    This hypothesis is supported by the experimental evidence that the thickness of ASR-induced P-SCO is significantly reduced compared with the initial BM-SCO sample (Figure 4b), indicating the dissolution of the thin film during the transition. In strong contrast, the P-SCO (with the Co octahedra, Cooct) is more stable against the acid solution, with the crystalline structure preserved for a time duration of ≈1min (Figure S4d, Supporting Information). While for longer soaking time (e. g., 5 min), the XRD peak for P-SCO totally disappears, which indicates that the whole sample is dissolved within the solution.
    This result demonstrates that the resistance of Cotet (Co tetrahedra) and Cooct to the acid are extremely different, and the Cotet is considered as an active site for acid dissolution.
    这一假设得到了实验证据的支持，即 ASR 诱导的 P-SCO 薄膜厚度与初始的 BM-SCO 样品相比显著减少（图 4b），表明在转变过程中薄膜发生了溶解。与此形成鲜明对比的是，P-SCO（具有 Co 八面体结构，Cooct）在酸性溶液中更为稳定，其晶体结构在约 1 分钟的时间内得以保持（支持信息中的图 S4d）。然而，当浸泡时间更长（例如 5 分钟）时，P-SCO 的 XRD 峰完全消失，这表明整个样品在溶液中溶解了。这一结果表明，Cotet（Co 四面体）和 Cooct 对酸的抵抗能力极为不同，Cotet 被认为是酸溶解的活性位点。

    For this redox reaction, the BM-SCO reacts with the protons, and half of the Co ions are reduced to dissolvable Co2+, while the other half is oxidized into Co4+ in the form of P-SCO. To verify this mechanism, we measured the elemental concentration within the solution using inductively coupled plasma-Mass Spectrometry (ICP-MS). We chose 4 mL sulfuric acid (c[H+] =
    0.01 mol L−1) as acid solution and then soaked the BM-SCO film (with a thickness of 26 nm and an area of 25 mm2) within the solution for 15 s. The subsequent ICP-MS measurements reveal a substantial amount of Co ions (2715 nmol L−1) within the acid solution after the reaction, which is consistent with the theoretical value of 2269 nmol L−1 calculated based on our proton-assist ionic disproportionation model.
    对于这一歧化反应，BM-SCO 与质子发生反应，其中一半的 Co 离子被还原为可溶的 Co2+，而另一半则被氧化为以 P-SCO 形式存在的 Co4+。为了验证这一机制，我们使用电感耦合等离子体质谱法（ICP-MS）测量了溶液中的元素浓度。我们选择了 4 毫升的硫酸（c [H+] = 0.01 mol L−1）作为酸性溶液，然后将 BM-SCO 薄膜（厚度为 26 纳米，面积为 25 平方毫米）浸泡在溶液中 15 秒。随后的 ICP-MS 测量结果显示，反应后酸性溶液中存在大量的 Co 离子（2715 nmol L−1），这与我们基于质子辅助离子歧化模型计算的理论值 2269 nmol L−1 相一致。

    In summary, we have successfully demonstrated two strategies, AAG and ASR, to achieve the phase transition from BM-SCO to P-SCO at a considerable reaction rate. The magnetic and electrical properties in the obtained P-SCO thin films were systematically investigated, and their close correlations with the valence state of Co ions and oxygen vacancy concentration were revealed.
    总之，我们成功展示了两种策略，AAG 和 ASR，以在相当的反应速率下实现从 BM-SCO 到 P-SCO 的相变。系统研究了所得 P-SCO 薄膜的磁性和电学特性，并揭示了它们与 Co 离子价态和氧空位浓度之间的密切关系。
